## Hi, I'm Luca!

 I’m passionate about using open source data for good - investigating issues that matter and telling those stories visually. 

 I recently graduated with a Master’s in Data Science for Public Policy from the [Hertie School](https://www.hertie-school.org/en/mds) and am building my work around OSINT, geospatial analysis and data visualization. 

 Explore some of my [projects](https://lucavellage.github.io/) or get in touch!
